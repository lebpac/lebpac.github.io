About the PAC

The LEB PAC (Parent's Advisory Council) works with the admin and teachers to improve school life and educational opportunities for our kids.

We organize fun activities (Welcome Back BBQ in September, Halloween Haunt in October, Bike to School Week, ...). We run babysitting and first aid courses for older grades. We run a choir program, and after school activities (chess, drawing, coding, dance, cooking, ...). We invite in interesting speakers and guests (circus, dance performances, authors, artists, ...).

We also run a hot lunch program, currently Tuesday (sushi) and Thursday (pizza), which are both fundraisers and a break from making lunch every day for parents!

The PAC also funds longer term projects. This year, we started EarthBites which is a food literacy program (all the way from soil preparation, through planting, harvesting, and preparation).

We would encourage you to get involved wherever you are able. It makes for a much richer school experience for both you and your children.

Evans Lake

Especially if you're a family that's joining us in Kindergarten, we'd like to encourage you to consider joining us on September Labour Day weekend at Evans Lake. It's a great way to get to know parents in the community. Having your Kindergarteners have some new best friends from camp the weekend before school starts makes for a much easier first week as well.
